Pragati Saccos Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What is a Cooperative Society?

A cooperative society is an autonomous association of persons united voluntarily to meet their common economic, social, and cultural needs and aspirations through a jointly-owned and democratically-controlled enterprise.

How can I become a member?

To become a member, you need to:

  1. Fill out the membership application form
  2. Submit required documents (ID proof, address proof)
  3. Pay the initial share capital
  4. Attend the orientation program

What are the benefits of membership?

Members enjoy several benefits including:

  • Access to financial services
  • Profit sharing
  • Voting rights
  • Educational and training opportunities
  • Networking with other members

Financial Services

How can I apply for a loan?

Loan applications can be submitted through our online portal or by visiting our office. You'll need to provide:

  • Completed loan application form
  • Income proof
  • Bank statements
  • Purpose of loan documentation

What are the interest rates?

Interest rates vary depending on the type of loan and current market conditions. Please visit our office or check the latest notices for current rates.

Meetings and Governance

When are the general meetings held?

General meetings are typically held quarterly, with the Annual General Meeting (AGM) taking place once a year. Members are notified in advance through notices and our website.

How can I participate in decision-making?

As a member, you can participate in decision-making by:

  • Attending general meetings
  • Voting on important matters
  • Standing for election to the board
  • Participating in committees
